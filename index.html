What is SF Draft?

SF Draft is a 5v5 character-based tactical FPS that emphasizes precise gunplay and strategic teamwork. Engage in matches where two teams of five players compete against each other in high-stakes scenarios.

Game Objective

The objective is to win matches by securing a total of at least 10 rounds. The main game mode features the Red Team attempting to plant a Bomb at one of two designated bomb sites (A or B), while the Blue Team works to prevent this or defuse the Bomb if it's planted.

Gameplay Highlights

Experience intense tactical gameplay with a focus on strategic planning and precision. Inspired by popular games, SF Draft brings a new level of excitement to the competitive FPS genre.
